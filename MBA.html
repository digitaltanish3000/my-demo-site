MBA Programs

1.Finance

MBA Finance is a post-graduate program that focuses on providing students with a strong foundation in finance and business management. The program covers a range of topics including accounting, financial management, investment management, corporate finance, economics, and risk management. Students who pursue an MBA in Finance typically gain a deep understanding of financial analysis and decision-making, and learn how to apply this knowledge to real-world situations. They also develop critical thinking, analytical, and communication skills that are essential in the business world.

  1. MBA Finance is a graduate-level degree program that focuses on the financial aspects of business management, including accounting, financial analysis, investments, and risk management.
  2. The curriculum of an MBA in Finance typically includes courses such as financial management, corporate finance, investments, financial statement analysis, and portfolio management.
  3. MBA Finance graduates can pursue careers in various industries, including banking, finance, investment management, consulting, and corporate finance.
  4. Some of the most popular job titles for MBA Finance graduates include financial analyst, investment banker, portfolio manager, financial manager, and risk manager.
  5. MBA Finance programs can be pursued full-time, part-time, or online, depending on the institution and the student's preferences.
  6. Placement Package:32 lakh per.annum

2.Marketing

An MBA in Marketing is a popular degree program that prepares students for careers in marketing management, advertising, brand management, and related fields. This program teaches students the fundamentals of marketing, including the development and execution of marketing strategies, market research, product positioning, and customer segmentation. Additionally, an MBA in Marketing provides a comprehensive understanding of consumer behavior, which is essential in developing effective marketing strategies. Students also learn how to create and manage strong brands, develop brand positioning, and understand the importance of brand equity. With the increasing use of digital technologies in marketing, an MBA in Marketing also equips students with knowledge and skills in digital marketing, including social media marketing, search engine optimization, and e-commerce. Finally, an MBA in Marketing provides students with the skills to analyze marketing data using marketing analytics, a critical skill for marketers in today's data-driven business environment. Graduates with an MBA in Marketing are well-equipped to pursue careers in a variety of industries, including consumer goods, technology, retail, and healthcare, among others.

  1. Marketing Strategy: MBA in Marketing teaches the fundamentals of marketing, including the development and execution of marketing strategies, market research, product positioning, and customer segmentation.
  2. Consumer Behavior: Understanding the behavior and psychology of consumers is crucial for effective marketing. An MBA in Marketing provides a comprehensive understanding of consumer behavior, which is useful in developing effective marketing strategies.
  3. Brand Management: Brand management is an essential aspect of marketing. MBA in Marketing equips students with the skills to create and manage strong brands, develop brand positioning, and understand the importance of brand equity.
  4. Digital Marketing: With the increasing use of digital technologies, digital marketing has become an essential part of modern marketing strategies. MBA in Marketing provides students with knowledge and skills in digital marketing, including social media marketing, search engine optimization, and e-commerce.
  5. Strategic Planning: MBA in Marketing teaches students how to develop and execute strategic marketing plans that align with organizational goals and objectives.
  6. Placement Package:22 lakh per.annum

3.Human Resource Management

MBA in Human Resource Management (HRM) is a specialized degree program that is designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills required to effectively manage human capital in an organization. The program covers various aspects of HRM, including recruitment, training and development, performance management, employee relations, compensation and benefits, and legal and ethical issues. Students are trained to develop a strategic mindset and problem-solving skills to manage human resources in a way that aligns with the overall business goals of the organization. The program provides a thorough understanding of the complex nature of human behavior in the workplace and equips students with the tools needed to build a positive and productive work culture. Graduates of MBA HRM programs can pursue a variety of careers in HR, such as HR manager, consultant, talent acquisition specialist, employee relations manager, compensation and benefits manager, and training and development manager. The demand for HR professionals is expected to increase as organizations recognize the importance of effective human capital management in achieving business objectives. MBA HRM programs are offered by many top-ranked business schools worldwide and can be completed in one to two years, depending on the program structure and format.

  1. MBA in Human Resource Management is a specialized degree program that focuses on developing skills and knowledge related to managing and leading human capital in an organization.
  2. The program includes courses in areas such as human resource planning, recruitment, training and development, performance management, employee relations, compensation and benefits, and legal and ethical issues in HR.
  3. Students in MBA HRM programs learn how to effectively manage and lead people to achieve organizational goals, and develop skills in problem-solving, decision-making, communication, and strategic planning.
  4. The degree is designed for professionals who wish to pursue careers in human resource management, as well as for those who are already working in the field and want to enhance their skills and knowledge.
  5. Graduates of MBA HRM programs can pursue a variety of careers, including HR manager, HR consultant, talent acquisition specialist, employee relations manager, compensation and benefits manager, and training and development manager.
  6. Placement Package:42 lakh per.annum

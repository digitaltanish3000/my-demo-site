BBA Programs

1.Banking and Finance Management

  1. Curriculum: The curriculum for a BBA Banking and Finance Management program typically includes courses in accounting, economics, financial management, marketing, organizational behavior, and business law.
  2. Industry certifications: Pursuing industry certifications such as the Certified Financial Planner (CFP) or Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) can enhance job prospects and earning potential for graduates.
  3. Further education: Some students may choose to pursue further education in the form of a Master's degree in finance, economics, or business administration.
  4. Accreditation: It is important to ensure that the BBA Banking and Finance Management program you choose is accredited by a recognized accrediting body.
  5. Career prospects: Graduates of BBA Banking and Finance Management can pursue careers in commercial banking, investment banking, asset management, financial planning, and corporate finance.
  6. Placement Package:12 lakh per.annum

2.Entrepreneurship and Family Businesses

  1. Collaboration: Collaboration is emphasized throughout the program, with students often working in teams on projects and assignments.
  2. Coursework: Coursework typically includes topics such as accounting, finance, marketing, management, entrepreneurship, family business management, and leadership.
  3. Career Opportunities: Graduates of the program can pursue a variety of career paths, including starting their own businesses, working in family businesses, or pursuing careers in management, consulting, or entrepreneurship.
  4. Internships: Many programs offer internships or other experiential learning opportunities to give students practical experience in entrepreneurship and family business management.
  5. Networking: Networking opportunities are often a key component of the program, allowing students to connect with successful entrepreneurs and family business owners.
  6. Placement Package:22 lakh per.annum

3.Human Resource Managemen

  1. MBA in Human Resource Management is a specialized degree program that focuses on developing skills and knowledge related to managing and leading human
  2. The program includes courses in areas such as human resource planning, recruitment, training and development, performance management, employee relations, compensation and benefits, and legal and ethical issues in HR.
  3. Students in MBA HRM programs learn how to effectively manage and lead people to achieve organizational goals, and develop skills in problem-solving, decision-making, communication, and strategic planning.
  4. The degree is designed for professionals who wish to pursue careers in human resource management, as well as for those who are already working in the field and want to enhance their skills and knowledge.
  5. Graduates of MBA HRM programs can pursue a variety of careers, including HR manager, HR consultant, talent acquisition specialist, employee relations manager, compensation and benefits manager, and training and development manager.
  6. Placement Package:22 lakh per.annum
